The Place

The place. It's where people can enjoy and relax. There is no need to rush. It can be your bedroom, library, or even on a bench. Here, it's a humble ramen shop. Our motto is simple: to provide customers a comfortable place to eat and enjoy after a hard day's work. Because at Matte, we want to remind you of home.
